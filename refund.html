NIFTY STEPS School Refund POLICIES

As a rule, Nifty Steps School does not make refunds to parents on fees already paid to the accounts of the school. However, (where within the first two weeks of a school term) extenuating circumstances, as may be determined by the school, warrant such refund, it will be treated on a pro rata basis. Request for fee refunds will not be entertained after the second week of any term.
