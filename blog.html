Our Mission Statement

The mission of Nifty Steps School is to provide a unique and distinctive education aimed at moulding each pupil to be a self-confident, inquiring, positive and tolerant person with a well-rounded character and an independent mind which will enable the child stand up for himself/herself and be of value to the larger community.

School History

Steps School started operation in October 2008 with the objective to provide a unique form of education to the Nigerian children. Our population has grown steadily from just five pupils and three teachers in the kindergarten class in 2008, to a current figure of 155 pupils and 21 teachers. Over 85% of these teachers are either NCE graduate or hold a degree in Education. Three others are currently in the middle of their NCE certification course. Future Plans for the School

We plan to expand our current 14 classrooms school block and construct a school hall, as well as establish language and music laboratories – so we can free up space and improve on our storage and retrieval ability.

INTRODUCTION


Nifty Steps College began operation as a secondary school in the 2013/2014 academic year with six (6) students who had just graduated from Nifty Steps Primary School. The school is currently a day secondary school, but there are plans to erect boarding facilities to meet the demands of several parents who want their children in a Nifty Steps College boarding house.

Be that as it may, our current day facilities are among the best among secondary schools. They boast of spacious classrooms, very clean toilets, as well as fully equipped science laboratories.

THE NIFTY STEPS college's mission STATEMENT

Our mission at Nifty Steps College is to provide a distinctive brand of education, aimed at molding the student to be a self-confident, inquiring, positive and tolerant person with a well-rounded character and an independent mind, all of which should enable him/her to contribute immensely to society at large.
Our education philosophy is better understood from the school’s commitment to positively shape the consciousness of every student that we guide through the learning process. In that vein, we emphasize that every student understand well the motto of the school which is cast in the Latin phrase, crescat scientia; vita excolatur; meaning that education or acquisition of knowledge enhances the quality of life. We teach our students the symbolic significance of all images in the school’s crest; stressing the implication of each image, and how like the eagle in the crest, for instance, the students are required to strive for excellence in all that they do in present and future climes and shores, carrying their acquired knowledge with them like an all-conquering sword.

OUR CORE VALUES


The core values that drive our activities at Nifty Steps College are:
-> Clarity
-> Collaboration
-> Respect
-> Thoroughness
-> Tolerance, and
-> Transparency

These core values govern our work ethics. They are the deeply shared convictions and beliefs upon which we justify our attitude to work, and by which our performances as individuals and as a team are judged by others. Arising from these six core values are our value statements that form the strategic standards against which we measure every action, every relationship and every piece of work that we engage in as players in the Nifty Steps College environment.
-> We collaborate with one another for optimum performance.
-> We keep communication short and clear to avoid ambiguities.
-> We respect the rights and person of the child, the teacher and the parent.
-> We are thorough in all that we do.
-> We are tolerant of the beliefs and the views of others, irrespective of their age, gender, background or nationality.
-> We are transparent in our relationships and in all our transactions.
