Welcome to Nifty Steps School

Children who have reached the age of 18 months are eligible for admission into PLAYGROUP class. We admit all year round, but we advice that parents seeking admission for their children within this category should plan for their child to join us during September admissions. It helps the child to flow along other children in the class.

All children coming to other Primary School classes will take a test to enable us determine the class into which the child would fit.

At the secondary school level, there is usually an entrance examination to Junior Secondary class 1, which can be done in either July or August of every year. Exact dates of the examination will be posted on this site. Your child should have done, at least, Basic 5, before attempting to join our JS1.

ADMISSION AND FEES

All prospective Nifty Steps College students are admitted on the basis of their performance in an entrance examination. Upon admission, the student is issued a list of requirements for the class into which he/she is admitted.

All charges for books and uniforms, as well as all other fees are to be paid by the parents, directly into the nominated school’s bank accounts. Nifty Steps College does not accept cash payments at the school for whatever purpose. Receipts for all payments made are issued upon the parent or guardian’s submission of a bank teller showing the amount paid.

As a rule, Nifty Steps College does not make refunds to parents on fees already paid to the accounts of the school. However, (where within the first two weeks of a school term) extenuating circumstances, as may be determined by the school, warrant such refund, it will be treated on a pro rata basis. Request for fee refunds will not be entertained after the second week of any term.
