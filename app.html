Our 2023 WASSC Examination

The picture above shows the best-graduating student in the 2023 WASSC Examination, Miss Moyinoluwa Atolagbe, as she received a commendation letter and cash award from the Director, Dr. Oladeji Egwaoje. Miss Moyinoluwa scored A1 in English Language, Mathematics, Further Mathematics, Physics, Data Processing, and Civic Education. Our 2023 WASSC results reflect the school’s emphasis on excellence in language and numeracy, among other subjects. Therefore, while the students obtained a 100 percent pass across the board in the examination, no candidate scored below C4 in English Language and Mathematics.

Our analysis reveals that 55 percent of the students scored A1, 40 percent scored B3, and the remaining five percent scored C4 in Mathematics. Whereas 90 percent of the students scored B3 and above in English, the remaining 10 percent had a C4 grade. Furthermore, all the students who offered Further Mathematics in the examination scored B3 and above.
